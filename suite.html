Cracka's Hash Burner v1.23

Strong Random Key & Secret Generator — GUID, Hex, Tokens & Mori BYOK Keys

A single standalone PowerShell tool — double-click the .bat and go, nothing to install. It burns cryptographically-secure random keys on demand and copies every one to your clipboard automatically. Wears the suite's neon Midnight Edition styling, with single-instance safety and a license gate.

Generation modes

  • Single key — exact character count, or a random length between a low/high bound.
  • Batch — burn many at once (fixed length or a per-key range); the whole set lands on the clipboard, newline-separated for Excel.
  • Custom-prefixed — lead every key with your own prefix (e.g. DJCRACKA-) for branded, traceable license keys and tokens.
  • Quick presets — one keystroke for a GUID/UUID, 32-char hex secret, 64-char alphanumeric token, or a 48-char Mori BYOK Master Key.

Character sets & length

  • Alphanumeric (A-Z a-z 0-9) — safest for API keys & URLs.
  • Alphanumeric + symbols — maximum entropy for master secrets & vault keys.
  • Hex (0-9 a-f) — the classic secret format.
  • Length — an exact count or a random count between two bounds, chosen per key.

Output & branded export

  • Auto-clipboard — every burn (single or batch) is copied instantly.
  • Branded Excel — export the last burn to a .xlsx with a frozen bold-header Hashes worksheet plus the signature DJ Cracka banner tab, saved to your Desktop with an auto-incrementing filename.

Security & theme

  • Crypto-secure RNG — not the ordinary pseudo-random; right for API secrets, tokens, webhook secrets and the Mori BYOK_MASTER_KEY.
  • Never transmitted — keys exist only in your session, your clipboard, and any Excel you choose to export.
  • Suite theme — randomized 5-color cyberpunk terminal, Midnight Edition banner, matching every other suite tool.
